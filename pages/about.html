About Us

I am Matthew Rungwe, a Web Developer at Embark. I have 2yrs of experience. In this time, I have been focusing greating on ways to improve and make it easy for beginners to learn web development and for experts to improve their way of developing websites and web applications.

Our Mission

The idea to develop Embark was to help beginners navigate their way safely to becoming good Web Developers. The concepts addressed cover the core, fundamental aspects of web development. During my learning, it took me a long time to understand what to focus on and what to leave behind. With time, I also realised that some concepts that were addressed later in my learning should have been more to the beginner's side than the expert.

For example, for HTML, concepts like responive design, semantics and accessibility should be the ideas at the forefront of or influencers for every HTML element you put on a web page and not to only be considered at the final stages of development or considered to be special case concepts.

Embark is a web development online learning platform. It is supported with a blog that focuses on best practices and updates in any of the technologies specialised. Careful research and hard work has been put in every word to ensure that what you learn co-relates to the general guidelines of learning and developing good websites and web applications.
